Fair Trade Matters

eTinda is built on the principles of fair trade. This means we ensure artisans receive fair compensation for their incredible work, providing them with stable incomes and empowering their communities. We support safe working conditions and sustainable production methods that respect the environment.

Sustainable Practices

We connect you with producers who use eco-friendly materials and traditional techniques. By purchasing through eTinda, you support sustainable livelihoods and contribute to reducing environmental impact, one beautiful product at a time.

From Farm to Table: Crafting Our Coffee

Learn how our artisanal coffee is made with care and tradition.

1. Harvesting the Cherries

In Batangas, farmers hand-pick ripe coffee cherries, ensuring only the best are selected for processing.

2. Drying the Beans

The cherries are sun-dried for weeks, a traditional method that enhances flavor while being eco-friendly.

3. Roasting with Care

Artisans roast the beans by hand, controlling the heat to bring out rich, aromatic notes.

4. Sustainable Packaging

The final product is packed in biodegradable bags, ready to bring joy to your mornings.
