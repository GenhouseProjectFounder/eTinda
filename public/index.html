Our Mission: Empowering Artisans, Sustaining Earth

eTinda is more than just a marketplace. We're a community dedicated to promoting fair trade practices, ensuring artisans receive fair wages and work in safe conditions. By choosing eTinda, you contribute directly to sustainable livelihoods and cultural preservation across the Philippines.

About eTinda

Learn how eTinda makes a difference.

Fair Trade Matters

eTinda is built on the principles of fair trade. This means we ensure artisans receive fair compensation for their incredible work, providing them with stable incomes and empowering their communities. We support safe working conditions and sustainable production methods that respect the environment.

Sustainable Practices

We connect you with producers who use eco-friendly materials and traditional techniques. By purchasing through eTinda, you support sustainable livelihoods and contribute to reducing environmental impact, one beautiful product at a time.
